# Beyond the Labels: Becoming Who God Says You Are

A faith centered online book experience written by Bradley Hobbs.

This project presents *Beyond the Labels: Becoming Who God Says You Are* as an immersive web based reading journey. Built with HTML and CSS, the site guides readers through themes of grace, identity, redemption, belonging, holiness, and hope while maintaining a unified visual atmosphere inspired by the book cover and banner design.

## Book Description

Beyond the Labels explores difficult questions surrounding identity, faith, rejection, and belonging through the lens of Scripture and grace.

Many people have carried labels placed on them by culture, experiences, or even church communities. This book points readers toward a greater truth:

Identity is not ultimately determined by human labels.

Identity is found in Christ.

Through themes of redemption, holiness, transformation, and hope, readers are invited into a journey beyond condemnation and toward the grace and purpose of God.
