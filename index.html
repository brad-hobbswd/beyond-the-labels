About This Book

Beyond the Labels explores questions many people quietly carry. Questions about belonging, rejection, identity, grace, faith, and hope.

Through Scripture, testimony, and practical encouragement, readers are invited beyond human verdicts and into the transforming love of Jesus Christ.

Labels may describe parts of a story, yet labels never determine destiny. Grace always speaks a stronger word.
